Featured Case Study

JPMorgan Chase Boosts Software Engineer Efficiency

JPMorgan Chase, a leading global financial services firm, was looking to improve the productivity of their software engineering teams while maintaining high code quality standards. Their development teams were spending significant time on routine coding tasks that could potentially be automated.

HEXA AIAGENCY implemented an AI coding assistant solution that could understand complex programming requirements, suggest code completions, and help with debugging. The system was trained on the bank's codebase and integrated with their development environment for seamless assistance.

20% Efficiency Increase
35% Faster Onboarding
15% Bug Reduction

The solution boosted software engineer efficiency by 10-20%, allowing the reallocation of resources to more strategic projects. New developers were onboarded 35% faster, and there was a 15% reduction in bugs found during code reviews. The engineering team now focuses on innovation rather than routine coding tasks.
